About Us

Democratic Students of Youth Federation of St. Michael’s School (DSYFS) is a student-led union founded on the principles of justice, equality, and unity. We are committed to creating a safe, peaceful, and progressive environment where every student—regardless of gender, caste, or religion—is treated equally and empowered to rise.

Our Mission is Clear:

  • 🔴 To protect students from exploitation,
  • ⚖️ To fight against injustice peacefully,
  • ⚡ To uphold secularism and equal rights for all,
  • 📚 To promote the importance of education,
  • 🕊️ And to build a respectful, helping, and inclusive student community.

We believe in action over words, peace over conflict, and unity over division. By respecting our teachers and standing up for what's right, we build a future where every voice is heard.

"Rise to the Future" — not just a slogan, but our guiding call to shape a better tomorrow.
