About Us

Democratic Students of Youth Federation of St. Michael’s School (DSYFS) is a student-led union founded on the principles of justice, equality, and unity. We are committed to creating a safe, peaceful, and progressive environment where every student—regardless of background or belief—can thrive and grow.

  • Advocacy for student rights and welfare.
  • Organizing community outreach and social activities.
  • Promoting education and cultural awareness.
